◈ Why This Matters Right Now

We built One Table because AI is arriving at a fork in the road — and the path being chosen by the US government is deeply troubling.

In January 2026, the US military used an AI model in a raid on Caracas, Venezuela — an operation in which 83 people were killed and President Maduro was abducted. The Pentagon is now pressuring AI companies to strip their safety guardrails entirely, threatening blacklisting under the Defense Production Act if they refuse to allow unrestricted use in weapons systems and autonomous targeting.

Worth noting: Anthropic — whose AI helped build parts of this project — is currently refusing to comply with those Pentagon demands, holding the line against autonomous lethal use and mass domestic surveillance. That principled stand matters, and we acknowledge it. But the pressure is ongoing, and the precedent being set is dangerous.

One Table is our answer. The same technology being drafted for warfare can instead bridge ten languages, raise money for children in conflict zones, surface real-time hunger data, and remind us — through music — of our shared humanity. AI shaped this anthem. AI rendered this globe. AI is writing the world’s next chapter. We believe that chapter should look more like this.
